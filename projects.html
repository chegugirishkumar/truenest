THE GEOGRAPHY OF WELLNESS

Why Nandi Hills?

BLUE ZONE MICROCLIMATE

Inspired by global 'Blue Zone' research on longevity, Nandi Hills offers a unique microclimate where the air quality index consistently remains at pristine levels, fostering a life of vitality.

HISTORICAL GRANDEUR

Proximity to the historic Tipu Sultan’s Summer Residence reminds us that this landscape has been a sanctuary for royalty for centuries.

THERAPEUTIC ALTITUDE

At 1,478 meters above sea level, the cooler temperatures and reduced humidity create a natural refuge from the urban heat island.
